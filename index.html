Building a Legacy of Excellence

Background

The Company was established in 2018 and is a general contractor offering construction & consultancy services in terms of Site Analysis, Feasibility Studies, and Preliminary Design Studies, in all construction building, Highway and Bridges and Water & Sanitation etc. We handle various residential, industrial, distribution, manufacturing, office, retail, recreational, healthcare and commercial projects in the Kampala Uganda area and East Africa regional markets.

Company is a leader in providing value-added construction services to our customers by creating a successful partnership with them throughout the construction process. Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our clients by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance by every member of the construction team.

Our Mission

Dedicated to providing quality construction, technical and management services to our clients. We strive to implement a long term relationship with our clients, based on safety, quality, timely service and an anticipation of their needs.
